Nostalgia

Follow a mother's search for the sounds that resonate the past and recreate the music that reminded her of what once was.

Nostalgia places the player in the eyes of a lone mother wandering in a post-apocalyptic world. She gathers sound signals coming from various sources and arrange them to recreate the music she unconsciously hears. With the recreation of music comes the reconstruction of her memories with her daughter. Little did she know what these signals really mean.

Personal Notes

This is an experimental project that really puts forward the team’s adaptability with new tech. We were so used to doing 2D games made in Unity, that the sudden change of technical requirements – Unreal Engine game in 3D – posed quite a challenge on us. Nevertheless, the team surpassed it through this simple game that showcases our growing talent.
